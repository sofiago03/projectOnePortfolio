These are a Few Artists Who Inspire Me

Guweiz

Guweiz is a digital artist who creates these fabulous world building designs focusing on two Japanese women who are assassins. I admire his ability to create in depth scenarios that these women face throughout his drawings. His attention to detail is another aspect of his art that I admire greatly because he thinks of it all.

The Marias

The Maria's are a band based in LA that makes latin indie pop music. Their last album Submarine was written together as a band and it came out to be very emotional and vulnerable from varying points of view from each member. I admire their ability to write music that speaks volumes and can relate to a lot of different people despite their backgrounds. The lead singer Maria inspires me to be more creative and to live in the now, not 5 minutes ago, yesterday, or tomorrow, but now.

Vivienne Westwood

Vivienne Westwood is an iconic english designer who wasn't afraid to use loud colors or patterns in her designs. Her clothing lines would alwyas include dramatic silhouettes with a blend of wavy lines, and harsh strick geometrical lines. Whenever I'm doing a project that I have to use more than a few basic colors on, I think of Vivienne's designs and how I can challenege color theory in a good way.
